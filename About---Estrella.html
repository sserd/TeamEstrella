About Estrella

Internship and Project Division (IPD) - 4

We are Team Estrella. In the course of 6 weeks, 15 students collectively trying to address and analyze the phenomenon of stellar chromospheric enhancement of the host star due to the presence of hot Jupiters in elliptical orbits To achieve this goal, the team read numerous papers by various authors like A.F.Lanza, S.Preusse, T.McIvor and various others. Upon studying them, we chose the A.F.Lanza model, and decided to modify it, and ran analytics via PYTHON.
